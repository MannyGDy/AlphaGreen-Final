Bio

Oladimeji, is a qualified accountant with over thirteen years of hand-on experience in research, banking, accounting and financial reporting, auditing, taxation, IFRS conversion, management consultancy and financial advisory services. He has worked in various Operations of banking industry including international remittances operations of Oceanic bank (NOW ECO bank).

He also worked with Ademola Omolehinwa & co where he rose to principal manager in A udit, tax and assurance. He is a season facilitator having facilitated at Excellent Associate, foremost ICAN preparatory centre in Ijebu Igbo Ogun State and Oceanic bank training school and currently senior lecturer in ADRAC Professional, A J Silicon, International School of Management, Brain work Limited, RISK FREE STANDARDS ASSOCIATES LIMITED and other leading accountancy and finance tuition centres in Nigeria. He is equally a guest lecturer at ICAN MCPE programme.

He is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountant of Nigeria (ICAN), member of ACCA Diploma in International Financial reporting, Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria (ACTI), Institute of Cost Management of Nigeria (ICMN), Associate Member of Accounting Technician Scheme and currently in his second stage of Chartered Financial Analyst examination.

Oladimeji, former president of Nigeria Accounting student Association, Ogun state university chapter, is a second-class upper graduate of Accountancy from Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ogun state.
